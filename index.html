Governing assumptions
  1. The record presented is complete. There are no unrecorded instruments.
  2. All instruments are valid, properly executed, acknowledged, delivered, and timely recorded.
  3. Texas law governs. Intestate succession is determined under the Texas Estates Code in effect at the relevant time.

  5. All parties are of legal age and capacity.
  6. Any interest acquired by a married person by gift, devise, or descent is separate property; all other interests acquired during marriage are presumed community property.

  8. Express all fractional interests as decimals carried to eight (8) places. Interests for each tract must total 1.00000000.
